# SkillSwap Connect

## Overview
SkillSwap Connect is a peer-to-peer skill exchange platform where users can teach and learn skills without money involved - just time credits. Instead of paying for lessons, you earn "time credits" by teaching others and spend them to learn new skills from your community.

## Problem Solved
- Traditional tutoring/lessons are expensive and inaccessible
- People have valuable skills they want to share but no way to monetize
- Language barriers prevent meaningful cultural exchange
- One-sided learning relationships (always paying or always teaching)

## Target Audience
- Students looking for affordable education
- Professionals wanting to diversify skill sets
- Retirees with expertise to share
- Immigrants seeking language practice
- Anyone interested in community building

## Key Features
1. **Time Banking System** - Earn 1 credit per hour taught, spend credits to learn
2. **Skill Matching Algorithm** - AI-powered matching based on interests, availability, and location
3. **Video Lesson Library** - Record and share lessons for asynchronous learning
4. **Community Forums** - Discussion boards for each skill category
5. **Rating & Review System** - Build trust through verified teaching/learning experiences

## Monetization Strategy
- Premium features: Advanced analytics, unlimited video storage, priority matching
- Verified instructor badges (subscription-based)
- Corporate partnerships for employee upskilling programs
- Featured listings in search results

## Tech Stack Suggestions
- **Frontend**: React Native (cross-platform mobile), Next.js (web)
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express or Python FastAPI
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (relational data), Redis (caching)
- **Other**: WebRTC (video calls), AWS S3 (file storage), Firebase (notifications)
