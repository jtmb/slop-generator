# MealMatch AI

## Overview
MealMatch AI is an intelligent meal planning and grocery shopping assistant that uses AI to create personalized weekly meal plans based on your dietary preferences, budget constraints, and nutritional goals. It automatically generates optimized shopping lists from local stores and integrates with delivery services for convenience.

## Problem Solved
- **Time-consuming meal planning**: Most people struggle to decide what to cook each week
- **Food waste**: People often buy ingredients they don't use or forget about them
- **Nutritional imbalance**: Many diets lack proper nutrition without conscious effort
- **Budget management**: Grocery shopping can be expensive without proper planning
- **Lack of variety**: Repeating the same meals gets boring

## Target Audience
- Busy professionals who want healthy meals but no time to plan
- Health-conscious individuals looking for balanced nutrition
- Families managing grocery budgets
- People with specific dietary restrictions (vegan, keto, gluten-free, etc.)
- Students and young adults learning to cook on a budget

## Key Features
1. **AI-Powered Meal Planning**: Generates personalized weekly meal plans based on preferences, dietary restrictions, and nutritional goals
2. **Smart Shopping List Generation**: Automatically creates optimized shopping lists grouped by store sections with estimated costs
3. **Recipe Database**: Access to thousands of recipes with filtering options (calories, ingredients, difficulty, cuisine)
4. **Budget Tracking**: Monitors spending against your grocery budget and suggests cost-saving alternatives
5. **Waste Reduction Alerts**: Reminds you to use ingredients before they expire and suggests recipes based on what you already have

## Monetization Strategy
- **Freemium Model**: Free basic meal planning with limited recipes; Premium ($4.99/month) for unlimited access, advanced features, and integrations
- **Affiliate Commissions**: Earn commissions from grocery delivery service partnerships and recipe ingredient affiliate links
- **Premium Recipes**: Curated chef-created recipes and specialized diet plans available as one-time purchases
- **Family Plans**: Discounted pricing for households with multiple users

## Tech Stack Suggestions
- **Frontend**: React Native or Flutter for cross-platform mobile app; React.js for web dashboard
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express or Python FastAPI for API services
- **Database**: PostgreSQL for structured data (users, recipes, orders); Redis for caching
- **AI/ML**: Python with TensorFlow/PyTorch for meal recommendation algorithms and natural language processing
- **Other**: 
  - Stripe for payment processing
  - Google Maps API for store location integration
  - SendGrid for email notifications
  - AWS S3 for recipe images and user data storage
