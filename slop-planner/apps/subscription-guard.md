# SubscriptionGuard

## Overview
SubscriptionGuard is a comprehensive subscription management platform that helps users track, monitor, and optimize their recurring subscription spending across all platforms. It provides centralized tracking with smart alerts and one-click management capabilities to help users save money on their monthly recurring charges.

## Problem Solved
Users lose track of recurring subscriptions, leading to forgotten renewals and unnecessary monthly expenses. There is no centralized way to monitor, manage, and optimize subscription spending across all platforms (streaming services, apps, boxes, memberships, etc.). People often forget about free trials that convert to paid subscriptions, miss price increase notifications, or don't realize they're paying for duplicate services.

## Target Audience
Tech-savvy professionals aged 25-45 who subscribe to multiple services (streaming, SaaS, delivery apps) and want to optimize their monthly spending without manual tracking. This includes freelancers, remote workers, families with multiple subscriptions, and anyone managing 10+ recurring charges.

## Key Features
1. **Centralized Dashboard**: Track all subscriptions with auto-discovery via bank statement analysis and manual entry
2. **Smart Alerts**: Notifications for upcoming renewals, price increases, and free trial expirations (email, push notifications, SMS)
3. **One-Click Management**: Pause, skip, or cancel recurring charges directly from the app with integrated payment processing
4. **Price Change Tracking**: Monitor subscription price fluctuations across platforms and get notified before price hikes
5. **Spending Analytics**: Visual insights into subscription spending patterns, monthly totals, and savings opportunities over time

## Monetization Strategy
- **Freemium Model**: Free basic tracking for up to 3 subscriptions; Premium ($4.99/month) for unlimited subscriptions, bank integration, and advanced analytics
- **Affiliate Revenue**: Partner with services offering discounts or free trials for users who cancel through the platform
- **Enterprise Tier**: Business subscription management for companies ($29/user/month) with team collaboration features and expense reporting

## Tech Stack Suggestions
- **Frontend**: React Native (cross-platform mobile app), React.js (web dashboard), Tailwind CSS for styling
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express or Python FastAPI, GraphQL API layer
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (relational data), Redis (caching and real-time alerts)
- **Other**: Plaid API (bank statement analysis), Stripe API (payment processing), AWS Lambda (serverless functions), SendGrid (email notifications), Firebase Cloud Messaging (push notifications)
