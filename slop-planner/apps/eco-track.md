# EcoTrack

## Overview
EcoTrack is a comprehensive carbon footprint tracking application that gamifies sustainable living. Users can log their daily activities, track environmental impact, participate in community challenges, and earn rewards for eco-friendly choices.

## Problem Solved
Many people want to live more sustainably but lack:
- Clear visibility of their personal carbon footprint
- Motivation to maintain sustainable habits
- Community support and accountability
- Understanding of how small changes add up over time

## Target Audience
- Environmentally conscious individuals aged 18-45
- Families looking to reduce their household impact
- Corporate employees participating in sustainability initiatives
- Students and young professionals interested in climate action

## Key Features
1. **Activity Logging**: Easy-to-use interface for logging transportation, food, energy usage, shopping, and other carbon-emitting activities with automatic calculations
2. **Personal Dashboard**: Visual charts and graphs showing carbon footprint trends over time, with personalized reduction goals and progress tracking
3. **Gamification System**: Earn "EcoPoints" for sustainable choices, unlock achievements/badges, compete in weekly challenges, and climb leaderboards
4. **Community Challenges**: Join group challenges (e.g., "No Meat Monday", "Zero Waste Week"), share tips with others, and participate in local sustainability events
5. **Smart Insights & Recommendations**: AI-powered suggestions for reducing carbon footprint based on user behavior patterns, with actionable tips tailored to individual habits

## Monetization Strategy
- **Freemium Model**: Free basic tracking; Premium ($4.99/month) includes advanced analytics, unlimited history, personalized coaching, and exclusive challenges
- **Corporate Partnerships**: B2B SaaS for companies wanting to track employee sustainability metrics and CSR reporting
- **Affiliate Marketing**: Eco-friendly product recommendations with affiliate commissions
- **Carbon Offset Marketplace**: Partner with verified carbon offset providers for users who want to neutralize their footprint

## Tech Stack Suggestions
- **Frontend**: React Native (cross-platform mobile app) or Flutter
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express or Python FastAPI
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (relational data) + Redis (caching/leaderboards)
- **Other**: 
  - TensorFlow/PyTorch for AI recommendations
  - Chart.js/D3.js for data visualization
  - Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications
  - Stripe for payments and subscriptions
