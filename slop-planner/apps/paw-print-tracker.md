# PawPrint Tracker

## Overview
PawPrint Tracker is a comprehensive pet health management app that helps pet owners keep track of vaccination records, vet visits, medication schedules, and overall health history in one organized digital place.

## Problem Solved
Pet owners struggle to maintain organized records of their pet's medical information, leading to missed vaccination appointments, forgotten medication schedules, and difficulty accessing important health history during emergencies or vet visits.

## Target Audience
Pet owners (dogs, cats, small animals) who want to maintain organized and up-to-date health records for their pets, particularly those with multiple pets or complex medical needs.

## Key Features
1. Automated vaccination & medication reminder system with customizable schedules
2. Digital health records storage including vet visit history, lab results, and treatment notes
3. Smart appointment booking integration with local veterinary clinics
4. Pet profile management for multiple pets in one household
5. Health journal to track symptoms, behavior changes, and wellness observations

## Monetization Strategy
- Freemium model: Basic tracking features free, premium subscription ($4.99/month or $49.99/year) for advanced features
- Affiliate partnerships with pet supply stores and veterinary services
- Optional in-app purchases for specialized health reports and analytics

## Tech Stack Suggestions
- **Frontend**: React Native (cross-platform mobile app), Flutter alternative
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express or Python FastAPI
- **Database**: PostgreSQL for structured data, Firebase for real-time features
- **Other**: Twilio for SMS reminders, Google Maps API for vet location services, AWS S3 for document storage
